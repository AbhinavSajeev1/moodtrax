# MoodTrax

Mood tracking app - log your daily mood (1-10) with an optional note, and view your mood trends over time on a graph.

Visit the app here: [moodtrax.vercel.app](https://moodtrax.vercel.app)

## Features
- Log mood entries with mood ratings (1-10) and optional notes about your day
- View, edit, and delete past entries 
- Visualize mood trends over time on a line graph
- Secure accounts to keep your mood data private (sign up/log in)
